What are Growth Funds?

Growth funds are a type of mutual fund that aims to achieve capital appreciation by investing in growth stocks. These funds invest primarily in companies that are expected to grow at an above-average rate compared to other companies. Growth funds are best suited for investors who are willing to take on more risk in exchange for the potential for higher returns over the long term.
