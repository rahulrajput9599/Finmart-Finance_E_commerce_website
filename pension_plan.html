Pension Plan Information

What is a Pension Plan?

A pension plan is a retirement plan that provides a specified monthly income to retired employees for the rest of their lives.

Comparison of Different Pension Plans

Plan Type Eligibility Contribution Structure Benefit Payment
Defined Benefit Pension Plan Employer-sponsored; often for long-term employees Employer-funded; based on salary and years of service Guaranteed monthly income for life
Defined Contribution Pension Plan Employer-sponsored; often for all employees Employee-funded or matched contributions Income based on contributions and investment returns; not guaranteed
Government Pension Plan For government employees (e.g., Social Security in the US) Funded through taxes or employee contributions Guaranteed monthly income or benefits
