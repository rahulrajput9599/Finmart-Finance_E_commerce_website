Understanding Auto Insurance

Auto insurance provides financial protection against physical damage and/or bodily injury resulting from traffic collisions and against liability that could also arise from incidents in a vehicle. It can also offer financial protection against vehicle theft, and against damage sustained from events other than traffic collisions, such as keying, weather or natural disasters, and damage sustained by colliding with stationary objects.
