Understanding Home Insurance

Home insurance, also commonly called homeowner's insurance, is a type of property insurance that covers a private residence. It provides financial protection against disasters. A standard policy insures the home itself and the things you keep in it. Home insurance covers damage to your property and your liability or legal responsibility for any injuries and property damage you or members of your family cause to other people.
