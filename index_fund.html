What are Index Funds?

Index funds are a type of mutual fund or exchange-traded fund (ETF) designed to replicate the performance of a specific index, such as the S&P 500. These funds aim to match the performance of the market index by holding all or a representative sample of the securities in the index. Index funds offer a low-cost, diversified investment option suitable for long-term investors seeking steady growth.
