401k Retirement Plan Information

What is a 401k Plan?

A 401k plan is a retirement savings plan sponsored by an employer. It lets workers save and invest a piece of their paycheck before taxes are taken out. Taxes aren't paid until the money is withdrawn from the account.

Comparison of Different 401k Plans

Plan Type Employer Match Tax Treatment Contribution Limits
Traditional 401k Yes Pre-tax $19,500 (Under 50), $26,000 (50 and over)
Roth 401k Yes After-tax $19,500 (Under 50), $26,000 (50 and over)
Solo 401k No Pre-tax or After-tax $58,000 (Under 50), $64,500 (50 and over)
