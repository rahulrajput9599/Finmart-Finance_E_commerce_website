Individual Retirement Plans (IRAs) Information

An Individual Retirement Account (IRA) is a savings account with tax advantages that individuals can use to invest for retirement. There are several types of IRAs:

  • Traditional IRA
  • Roth IRA
  • SEP IRA (Simplified Employee Pension)
  • SIMPLE IRA (Savings Incentive Match Plan for Employees)

Each type of IRA has different eligibility criteria, contribution limits, and tax implications. Compare the different IRAs before selecting one for your retirement savings.
