What are Balance Funds?

Balance funds, also known as hybrid funds, are investment funds that invest in a mix of asset classes, typically a combination of stocks and bonds. These funds aim to provide investors with a balanced portfolio that offers both growth and income opportunities while managing risk. Balance funds are suitable for investors seeking diversification and a moderate level of risk.
