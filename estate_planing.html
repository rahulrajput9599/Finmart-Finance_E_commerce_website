Estate Planning Details

Estate planning is the process of arranging for the management and distribution of your assets after your death. It involves making decisions about who will inherit your property, managing estate taxes, and appointing guardians for minor children.

A comprehensive estate plan can help ensure that your wishes are carried out and that your loved ones are provided for after you're gone. It may include a will, trusts, powers of attorney, and other legal documents.
