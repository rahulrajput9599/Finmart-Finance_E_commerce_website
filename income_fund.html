Income Funds

What are Income Funds?

Income funds are a type of mutual fund that aims to provide regular income to investors by investing in income-generating securities such as bonds, dividend-paying stocks, and other fixed-income assets. These funds are designed for investors looking for a steady income stream rather than capital appreciation.
